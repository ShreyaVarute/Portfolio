Demonstrating Fiber Optic Communication

Optical fiber communication is a pivotal technology in modern telecommunications, enabling data to be sent over long distances with significantly higher speed and lower loss compared to traditional wired communications. This project aims to construct a basic optical communication link to explore and demonstrate the technology's core mechanics and applications, making it accessible and understandable at an educational level.
